Design and Production Methodology

This premium cable knit wool texture was created through a meticulous multi-stage process:

  1. High-resolution photographic capture of real hand-knitted Merino wool fabric using specialized macro photography equipment
  2. Advanced photogrammetry techniques to capture precise three-dimensional surface detail
  3. Careful digital cleanup to ensure perfect seamless tiling while preserving natural fiber characteristics
  4. Color calibration against physical Pantone standards to ensure accurate reproduction across different rendering platforms
  5. Procedural enhancement to optimize the texture for scaling from close-up detail to distant tiling without quality loss
  6. Comprehensive testing across multiple 3D applications and rendering engines to ensure consistent results
