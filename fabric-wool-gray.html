Detailed Visual Characteristics

The light gray felted wool texture features a dense, non-directional surface with these distinctive qualities:

  • Uniform, compressed fiber structure with microscopic surface variations
  • Subtle marled appearance from the blending of slightly different toned wool fibers
  • Precise color calibration matching Pantone 14-4102 TCX "Glacier Gray"
  • Random short fibers visible at close inspection, creating natural surface noise
  • Low-contrast, non-repeating pattern ensuring natural appearance even when tiled across large surfaces
  • Minimal spectral highlights consistent with the light-absorbing properties of wool
  • Even density distribution with slight variations in thickness producing natural shadow-catching contours

Design and Production Methodology

This premium light gray felted wool texture was created through a methodical process:

  1. High-resolution photographic capture of industrial grade wool felt using controlled lighting conditions and polarized filters
  2. Multi-angle surface scanning to capture complete three-dimensional characteristics
  3. Advanced digital processing to ensure perfect seamless tiling while preserving natural material variations
  4. Manual refinement of each PBR map to ensure physically accurate material behavior
  5. Extensive testing across multiple rendering platforms to verify consistent appearance and behavior
  6. Optimization for efficient GPU rendering while maintaining ultra-high detail levels
